Welcome to Kakanj!

A small town with a lot to offer!

Visit Kakanj and convince yourself that not only big cities are entertaining!

What you need to know about Kakanj:

Kakanj is a municipality in BiH.

Located in the center of the country.

Number of residents: 37 441

Canton: Zeničko-dobojski

Municipality area: 462 km 2

Popular places to see:

Kraljeva Sutjeska

Visit one of the oldest mosques in our country which was build in 1463. when Ottoman Sultan Fatih was going to conquer Bobovac! Small, traditional mosque that brings inner peace!

View more

Old town Bobovac

Bobovac is a fortified city of medieval Bosnia and Herzegovina. Located near today's Vareš and the village of Borovica. It is a protected site as a National monument of Bosnia and Herzegovina.

View more

Franciscan friary

Kraljeva Sutjeska is a Bosnian Franciscan monastery in Bosnia and Herzegovina. It includes a museum in which it houses historical treasures, a library which keeps rare and valuable historical records.

View more

Ponijeri mountain

Beautiful mountain located 20km from the center of Kakanj, and on the height of 1100 meters above the sea level. Its Popular tourist spot especially during winter season.
