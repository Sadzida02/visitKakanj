Malak Farm

This farm in Kakanj offers accommodation in nature, delicious food, but also numerous facilities that will delight the whole family! If you are looking for a perfect vacation, just like the one in the countryside, we are not exaggerating when we say that this farm will win you over with each of its facilities! Namely, it is the Malak Farm located in Kakanj, but below you will see that it is not just a farm but much more. On their site you will be greeted with words that hint that this is about the whole experience. ,, Malak Farma is a small step towards making people aware and bringing people closer to their sense of existence, from the way they eat, to changing their relationship to the environment and generally understanding the principles of existence. Welcome, oh you wonderful people."

You can visit the Malak farm on a one-day tour, and if you want to experience this place further, they have accommodation for you. There are 3 types of accommodation: Malak Dream House, Malak Dream House Cube and Villa Malak Farma. Judging by the Instagram photos from this place, for many the decision falls on an unusual, modern but still in harmony with nature – Malak Dream House Cube. It is intended for two people with the most beautiful view of the farm. Inside there is a living room with a kitchen and a dining room, a bathroom and a bedroom that can be reached by the external stairs. In addition to the accommodation, we were also impressed by the choice of adrenaline activities in one place. You can enjoy zip-line, rock climbing, paintball, horse riding, walking on forest and educational trails and enjoy api therapy. At the end of this trip, rest in the restaurant of this farm, where you will be able to enjoy healthy and organic dishes, where almost all the ingredients are from this farm. The farm is located in Kakanj at Zajezda 5, and you will find all additional information on their website and Instagram page.
