Mountain Lodge Bočica

Mountain lodge Bočica is located at 920 meters above sea level, on the north-western slopes of the Zvijezda mountain, 10 km from Kakanj, in the central part of Bosnia and Herzegovina. The mountain lodge is managed by the Bobovac Mountaineering Association, which was founded in 1958. It was built in the eighties of the XX century and is open throughout the year. It is possible to reach the home by motor vehicle: (11 km) Kakanj-Zgošća-Zagrađe-Halinovići-Bočica (on the asphalt road to the village of Halinovići, and then to the home 2 km on a good macadam road).

It takes about 2 hours to reach the marked path. The home is surrounded by coniferous and coniferous forests. Next to the home, there are sports fields. The living room and dining room with kitchen have 60 seats and the necessary kitchen inventory for self-preparation. There are a total of 46 beds in double, quadruple and multi-bed rooms.[1] The development center of the Kakanj municipality in cooperation with the "Bobovac" mountaineering association Kakanj has created a project application for the project "Phase II construction of the winter garden at Bočica PD".

It includes the continuity of construction, in the initial phase of which the Municipality of Kakanj and Kakanj businessmen participated. The project meets the criteria of the Public Call for the collection of requests for approval and allocation of incentive funds intended to subsidize the construction and renovation of public tourist infrastructure, which was announced by the Ministry of Economy of the Zenica-Doboj Canton.[2] The project application was supported by the Municipality of Kakanj with co-financing of 21% of the total amount of the project. Every year, an event called "Dipijevstazama" is held in memory of the deceased member of PD "Bočica" Edib Dogdibegović - Dipija.
